Yunchao Liu

I am a fifth-year PhD student in the Theory Group at UC Berkeley, advised by Umesh Vazirani. My research interests are in quantum information and computation. Recently, I have been working on establishing theoretical foundations for near-term quantum advantage.

I received my Bachelor's degree from Yao Class at Tsinghua University, where I worked with Xiongfeng Ma.
